<objective>
Stand up the fpm project and the foundation/data-structure layer of the simulator, retiring
the throwaway Hello-World Makefile scaffold (D-08). This delivers the bottom of the acyclic
module stack — `kinds_mod` → `constants_mod` → `grid_mod` — plus the `fpm.toml` manifest with
explicit dev/release flag profiles (D-09), so every later plan compiles under the right flags.

Purpose: Without a clean fpm project and the foundation types, nothing else in the phase can
build. `grid_mod` defines the `type(cell)` that carries the GRID-02 per-cell fields as
`real(wp)`, and `coeffs_t` that the namelist loader will fill in 01-02.

Output: `fpm.toml`, `.gitignore`, updated `README.md`, `src/kinds.f90`, `src/constants.f90`,
`src/grid.f90`, a banner-only `app/main.f90`; retired `Makefile`/`src/numerics.f90`/`src/main.f90`
and a clean `build/`.
</objective>
